About Us

What is Career Karma?

A community of peers, mentors and coaches that will help you land a dream career in Tech.
You never pay a dime. The only cost is to help people behind you.

Career Karma is always free for students because after we coach you to prepare and get enrolled
into a bootcamp in our network, the school pays us a placement fee for helping you.
