Program Management

The organizations undertaking projects of magnitude like that of an ERP business transformation have realized the importance of effective program management for the org-wide initiative. This has brought forth formalized frameworks, methodologies to manage the projects effectively. Emicon delivers Project & Program Management Services to manage projects from planning stage to completion and further handover to in-house team.

Emicon offers following Project & Program Management Services:

  • Business Goals Alignment
  • Benefits Realization
  • Program Measurement
  • Project Governance
  • Organizational Change Management

Emicon further helps the organizations during the Project Initiation/Planning Process through following

  • Identifying the appropriate team structure
  • Identifying the key stakeholders and communicate the roles and responsibilities expected from every team member
  • Identify Expected Road Blocks and seek inputs from in-house team to overcome them
  • Prepare a road map from initializing to completion of vision
  • Break the road map into sizable projects to manage them effectively and reporting the status

Emicon helps in delivering the Transformation Optimization through following

  • Optimal use of Oracle Products and Technologies
  • Architectural Soundness
  • Optimal Performance in Production
  • Optimal Project Execution
